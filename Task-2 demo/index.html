ABOUT

AMETHYST is a highly anticipated event in the cultural calendar of Pune, India, and it's organized by the Cultural and Music Club of the Army Institute of Technology (AIT). The festival brings together a diverse group of people from different backgrounds and interests to showcase their talents, creativity, and enthusiasm for the arts.
The festival has always been known for its lively and energetic atmosphere, where students and visitors alike can participate in a variety of events, including music, dance, drama, fashion shows, and more.
AMETHYST is an opportunity for the students of AIT to showcase their creativity and talent in front of a large audience, and it's a chance for visitors to experience the rich cultural heritage of India.
